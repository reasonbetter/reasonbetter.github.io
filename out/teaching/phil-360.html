Phil 360: Global Priorities

Interdisciplinary course on the world’s most important problems and what we can do to help: (i) global health & poverty, (ii) animals & the environment, and (iii) emerging technologies. We also address how to compare problems, weigh human goods, and assess effectiveness.

Readings

I. Introduction & Mindset

  • MacAskill, Doing Good Better (Ch. 1–2) — Effectiveness framing
  • Manley, Reason Better (Ch. 1–2)

II. Meet the World

  • Rosling, Factfulness (selected chapters)
  • Our World in Data: Mortality in the Past
  • Dollar Street (Gapminder)

III. Hearts & Minds

  • Bloom, Empathy and its Discontents
  • Soares, On Caring
  • Slovic, The more who die, the less we care

IV. Extreme Poverty & Policy

  • Banerjee & Duflo, Poor Economics (selected)
  • 80,000 Hours: The good, the bad, and the ineffective

V. Catastrophic Risk & Emerging Tech

  • Founders Pledge: Climate change / Mental health (excerpts)
  • Future of Life Institute topic pages
